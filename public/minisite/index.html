The Smart Tracking card is a credit card sized tag which you attach to your travelling luggage and has a unique Safe Bag Code and barcode. Simply register the card online and activate it for the flight you will be boarding.

The Smart Tracking Code allows Safe Bag to contact you when it receives a SITA notification when your lost luggage is found!
